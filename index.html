Who I Am

About Me

I am a Video Editor, Content Creator, and Digital Marketer based in Austin, TX. I built two TikTok brands from zero to a combined 700K+ followers and 200M+ total likes across TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram — entirely through organic strategy, data-driven optimization, and relentless execution.

My background spans video editing, thumbnail design, paid social advertising, web development, and brand strategy. I have run Meta Ads campaigns for clients, redesigned websites from scratch, and grown audiences in highly competitive niches without a budget or a team.

I also work as a Voice Actor & Model through Model and Talent Management (MTM), developing on-camera presence, vocal delivery, and professional performance technique across commercial and digital media projects.

Before going all-in on content, I was a Division I soccer player at UW-Milwaukee and graduated from UW-Madison with a degree in Art and a Certificate in Video Editing & Graphic Design.
